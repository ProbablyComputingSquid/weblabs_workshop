grilled cheese

a recipe for a skillet-fried cheese sandwhich

By: Jasmine

Ingredients:






Instructions:

  1. Spread mayonaise on one side of each slice of bread.
  2. Place one slice of cheese on the un-mayonaised side of one slice of bread.
  3. If you are using ham, place the ham on top of the cheese.
  4. Place the second slice of cheese on top of the ham or first slice of cheese.
  5. Place the second slice of bread on top, mayonaise side up.
  6. Heat a skillet over medium heat.
  7. Place the sandwich in the skillet and cook until the bottom slice of bread is golden brown, about 3-4 minutes.
  8. Carefully flip the sandwich and cook until the other side is golden brown and the cheese is melted, about another 3-4 minutes.
  9. Remove from skillet, let cool for a minute, then slice and serve.
